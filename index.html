Charles Schwab: Digital Services Internship

In this role, I gained exposure to the agile framework and methodology as well as product management. I participated in weekly standups with scrum masters and saw first-hand the significance of having cross-functional teams. During this internship I utilized and grew my skills in: HTML, Excel, Powerpoint, Outlook, Confluence, Jira and Public Speaking. I had the opportunity to present to three high level directors and vice presidents. I shared more about this experience in an interview with my university here.

Resident Advisor (RA)

I was a Resident Advisor (RA) for my sophomore and junior year of college supporting our first-year students. I was responsible for the largest hall in my building and have been able to make a direct impact on over 80 students in total over that two-year time period. In those two years, some of the skills that were key to being a successful RA were: conflict resolution, decision-making during high-stress situations, relationship-building, and time management.

Microsoft: Product Advisor

In late Spring of my first-year in college, I had the opportunity to step away from my enjoyable role as a sales associate at Foot Locker, to join the team at Microsoft as a product advisor supporting and providing solutions to customers' problems ranging from deciding which Microsoft Office licenses to invest in for themselves and their business to even providing one-on-one support to an author completing their book to be published. My resposibilities went as far as supporting off-site demos and conducting summer workshops for youth ages 6-15.
